Welcome to ShepherdCare

ShepherdCare helps your pastoral team stay connected to the people in your congregation — tracking care visits, prayer requests, important milestones, and follow-up tasks, all in one place.

This guide walks you through the trial in just a few minutes.

💡
About this trial: Everything you see is sample data for a fictional church. You can click, explore, add notes, create tasks, and change statuses freely — nothing is saved permanently and nothing will affect a real church. Refresh the page at any time to start fresh.
Getting Started
1
👤 Choose a Role to Sign In As

On the opening screen you'll see six staff members to sign in as. Each one has a different role — from Senior Pastor to Volunteer — and each role sees a different version of the app. Start with Pastor David Mercer (Senior Pastor) to see everything, then try a Volunteer to see how limited access works.

💡 You can switch roles anytime by clicking your avatar in the top-right corner.
2
⌂ Explore the Dashboard

After signing in, you'll land on the Dashboard. This is your home base — it shows a quick summary of who needs attention, your open tasks, upcoming birthdays and anniversaries, and recent care activity. Click any stat card at the top to jump straight to that group of members.

3
✦ Browse the Congregation

Tap People in the navigation to see all congregation members. Filter by group (Seniors, Youth, Bereaved, etc.), by status, or use the ⛪ Members Only and 👋 Visitors Only options to separate regulars from first-time visitors. Tap any member to open their full profile — contact info, family details, care notes, and tasks are all there.

💡 Use the sort dropdown to arrange members A→Z by last name or by newest added.
4
📝 Add and Read Care Notes

Open any member's profile and tap the purple pencil button (✎) in the bottom-right corner to add a note. Choose a type — Visit, Hospital, Call, Prayer, and more — write what happened, and save. You can mark a note as private so only senior staff can see it. To read a full note, just tap it — it opens in a detail view. If you wrote the note, you'll see an ✏️ edit button to update it.

5
◻ Create and Complete Tasks

Go to Tasks in the navigation. Tasks are follow-up actions assigned to team members — things like "Follow up after hospital visit" or "Arrange transport for Sunday." Tap the checkbox on any task to mark it complete. Tap the + button to create a new one. You can assign a task to a specific person, leave it as To Be Assigned, or assign it to 👥 All Team Members so everyone sees it.

💡 Tasks are color-coded by priority — red is Urgent, yellow is High, blue is Medium.
6
🔍 Search Anything

Tap the 🔍 search icon in the top-right at any time to search across members and care notes instantly. Try searching a name, a group like "Seniors," a word from a note like "hospital," or even a child's name — the app will surface the parent's profile.

What's Inside
👥
Member Profiles

Full contact info, family details, spouse linking, children, groups, and care history. Members and visitors are tracked separately.

📋
Care Notes

Log visits, calls, hospital trips, prayer requests, and more. Tap any note to read it in full. Authors can edit their own notes. Private notes are visible only to senior staff.

✅
Task Management

Assign follow-up tasks to a specific person, leave them unassigned, or send them to all team members at once with priority levels and due dates.

🎂
Milestones

Never miss a birthday or anniversary. The app tracks upcoming dates and alerts you 30 days ahead.

📊
Reports

See congregation health at a glance — who's active, who needs care, and how each staff member is doing.

◈
Activity Feed

A chronological timeline of all care interactions across your entire pastoral team.

Staff Roles & Access
Senior Pastor
Full access — sees all members, all private notes, manages the team, and views all reports.
Co-Pastor
Broad access — all members, care notes, and reports. Cannot manage team accounts.
Care Pastor
All members and care notes. Cannot view private notes written by others.
Elder
Sees only their assigned members. Can add care notes and prayer entries.
Youth Minister
Assigned members only. Can edit profiles and add care notes for their group.
Volunteer
Most limited — sees only assigned members and can add basic care notes.
